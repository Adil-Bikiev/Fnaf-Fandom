The Fazbear Legacy: A Promise of Joy

Our flagship location, Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, quickly became the premier family entertainment destination, featuring our beloved animatronic band: Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie the Bunny, Chica the Chicken, and Foxy the Pirate Fox.
While the original location has since closed, our legacy continues through memorabilia, fan communities, and the enduring love for our characters. Certain... unverified stories have circulated over the years regarding technical malfunctions and unfortunate incidents. These are largely fictional tales born from overactive imaginations and the misuse of our brand in unauthorized "indie horror games."
Fazbear Entertainment maintains that all past incidents were thoroughly investigated and resolved. We look forward to future innovations in family entertainment. Remember: A smile is forever.
